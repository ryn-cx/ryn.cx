## Overview

Itamae is a userscript that lets you extract and preserve your Crunchyroll viewing
history. It runs in your browser through a userscript manager and exports your watch
data to JSON for local storage.

## Installation

Installation takes two steps. First, install [Tampermonkey](https://www.tampermonkey.net/)
or a similar userscript manager. Then install the Itamae script itself.

## How it works

Navigate to your Crunchyroll history page and use the "Load History" button to populate
your viewing records automatically, or scroll to the bottom of the page manually. Once
your history is loaded, click "Save History" to export your watch data as JSON.
