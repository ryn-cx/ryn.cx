## Overview

Preferred Difficulty is a Minecraft Fabric mod that lets each player set their preferred
difficulty and automatically adjusts the server's difficulty based on who is connected.
The mod tracks the preferred difficulty of every player on the server and applies the
easiest one chosen among them.

## Purpose

This mod was designed to fill a very specific purpose. It came out of a small, casual
Minecraft server running on normal difficulty for a group of friends, where some of them
wanted a harder difficulty so they could use specific farming setups, while others wanted
normal for a more relaxed play style. As a compromise, this mod lets the farms function most of the
time, but when a player who prefers an easier difficulty joins, the server automatically
changes to the difficulty they prefer. It also has a setting for a minimum difficulty
level, because setting the difficulty to peaceful can break some farm designs by causing
mobs to despawn.
